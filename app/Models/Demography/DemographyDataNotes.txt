Most reliable data source — OpenAfrica:
africaopendata.org or data.humdata.org
Search "South Africa municipalities" — these humanitarian data platforms often have clean CSVs of SA municipal data with multiple code systems already joined, specifically because NGOs and researchers face exactly your problem.

South African Government data portal:
data.gov.za
Search "municipal codes" or "demarcation" — Statistics SA publishes boundary and code correspondence files here in non-shapefile formats.
